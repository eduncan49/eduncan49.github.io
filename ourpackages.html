Our Specialty Packages

Babybird Postpartum Home Visits

Three or six home visits by an experienced postpartum RN to review the many skills that are introduced in the hospital, but can feel overwhelming once you’re home and trying to perform them on your own.

  • Baby Bath
  • Swaddling
  • Breastfeeding Assistance
  • Bottle feeding/Pace feeding
  • Nap breaks
  • Daily schedule organization assistance
  • Practical guidance and recommendations for trusted baby gear
  • Provide resources for community services as needed
  • Maternal emotional support

Our nurses can also do maternal blood pressure checks and newborn weight checks, if clinically indicated.

Maybe you’re a new mom and would like a little extra support as you get the hang of your new little love, or you’re a dad who wants some guided practice while mom gets a little rest. Perhaps this isn’t your first child, but it’s been a while since you bathed and swaddled a newborn, and you want to be up to date on the latest techniques and safety guidelines.

Our postpartum nurses are here to reinforce the teaching from the hospital, answer all your questions, and empower you to feel confident in caring for your baby, all in the comfort of your own home.
