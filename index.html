Our mission is to partner with patients and families to provide highly personalized healthcare navigation and extended support in order to achieve your goals of care.

Why Choose Hummingbird Consulting?

Times of health transitions can be unexpected and scary. Our specialized nurse navigation services are designed to streamline patient care and optimize outcomes. Healthcare navigation is easier and more efficient with a compassionate and knowledgeable nurse who shares your goals and can help alleviate the worry that comes with managing care.

Our Story

Hummingbird Concierge Nurse Consulting was born of my experience as the primary caregiver to my loved ones merged with my long-tenured career as a Registered Nurse at a premier Boston hospital.

    What We Do

Care Coordination

Coordinating follow-up care after hospitalization, a rehab stay or a new diagnosis

Guidance for Chronic Illness

Assist with the home management of chronic illness through regular assessments

Coaching

Coach family in advocating for patient’s needs while in the hospital and at home

Medication Management

Help you understand your medication routine and provide education for new prescriptions

Palliative Care

Discuss palliative care as a way to optimize comfort and improve quality of life

Hospice Support

Provide extended support and assistance

Community Services

Continuity of care through connections with home-based and community services

Extended Support Options

Providing support and guidance after covered services have ended

Home Check-ins

Peace of mind visits to assess health status and develop a plan for any areas of concern

Communication

Assist with communication with patient’s care team to achieve desired outcomes
