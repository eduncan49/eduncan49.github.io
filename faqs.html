Frequently Asked Questions


Is Hummingbird Concierge Nurse Consulting covered by Insurance?

Our services are private pay only. The services we offer are specific to your needs and tailored to your unique situation. We offer a highly personalized concierge approach in order to help you or your loved ones meet the established goals of care in a way that insurance does not cover.

How do I know if Hummingbird Consulting is right for me?

We offer an initial consultation to see how we can best help you manage whatever health concerns you or your loved ones are experiencing. The initial fee will be applied to any services you choose or plan you enroll in.

What is the cost?

We offer hourly and comprehensive packages starting at $150 an hour. Please contact us for more information and details.

Can a nurse go to an appointment with me?

Yes, we can arrange for a nurse to accompany you to an appointment to help you ask questions, advocate for you with your care team, clarify information, and relay it to your loved ones (if you want).

What are the benefits of Hummingbird Consulting?

We provide services that patients and families are desperately seeking, but are hard to find. Having a personal nurse navigator to coordinate new, complex or chronic health conditions, act as a compassionate, experienced sounding board for your concerns, and provide professional guidance will help alleviate the stress that comes with caregiving and improve your quality of life. Let us partner with you to provide exceptional support and guidance every step of the way.
